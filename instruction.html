Hi, Welcome

Firstly you will need to select Categories and Difficulty.

The Difficulty determines how hard the questions will be, and how much time will you have to answer the questions.

On the categories section you can choose one categigory or choose multiple categories from which the question will randomly come from.

The game ends when you click "next" button after the last Question. this will take you to the Score page.

You can check the correct answers by clicking the Review button on the score page
